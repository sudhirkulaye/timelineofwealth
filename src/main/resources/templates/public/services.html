Services and Features

  • For Retail Investors:

    • To get meaningful and up-to-date information about your wealth (a big picture) on one click

      • Audit Wealth, Income, Expenses, Savings, Liabilities, Liquidity Need, and Insurances
      • Monitor Current Asset Allocation to make appropriate changes suitable to the prevailing investment scenario

    • To seek unbiased advice from professionals

    • To maintain historical data points

      • To improve decision making
      • To estimate a lifestyle spending need
      • To evaluate retirement funding

    • Smooth transition while assigning a new fund manager/adviser

  • For Institutional Investors:

    • To maintain and track IPSs(Investment Policy Statement)

    • Get professional portfolio management services

      • Track cash-flows of portfolio
      • Assign Benchmark
      • Get both since inception and latest quarter TWRR and MWRR of portfolio

    • Smooth transition while assigning a new money manager

  • Investment Managers/Asset Management Firms:

    • To maintain and track IPSs(Investment Policy Statement)

    • To improve quality of advise/decisions related to client's wealth

    • To enhance efficiency concerning time per client

    • To provide the fastest, an accurate and economical communication channel for performance presentation

    • To provide standardized performance representation, both Money-Weighted Rate of Returns (MWRR) and Time Weighted Rate of Returns (TWRR)
    • To get assistance in GIPS compliance and thereby competitive advantage in the industry
