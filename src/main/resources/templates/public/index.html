About TimelineOfWealth

Timeline of wealth is a technology solution for
  • Retail Investors:
    • To get meaningful and up-to-date information about wealth (a big picture)
    • To seek unbiased advice from professionals
    • To maintain historical data points
      • To improve decision making
      • To estimate a lifestyle spending need
      • To estimate retirement funding
    • Smooth transition while assigning a new manager/adviser
  • Investment Managers/Asset Management Firms:
    • To maintain and track IPSs(Investment Policy Statement)
    • To improve quality of advise/decisions related to client's wealth
    • To improve efficiency in terms of time per client
    • To provide the fastest, an accurate and economical communication channel for performance presentation
    • To provide standardized performance representation, both Money-Weighted Rate of Returns (MWRR) and Time Weighted Rate of Returns (TWRR)
    • To get assistance in GIPS compliance and thereby competitive advantage in the industry
