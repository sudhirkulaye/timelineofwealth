Conclusions from Statistical Data:

Equity Market Returns are more volatile (riskier) for the shorter period. Standard Deviation, a measure of risk is the highest for 1-year returns and lowest for 10-year yields. In simple term longer the period you hold equity class is better.

There is also one consideration to this simplistic assumption of long-term holding.
