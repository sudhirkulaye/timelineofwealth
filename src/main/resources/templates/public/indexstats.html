Conclusions from Statistical Data:

Equity Market Returns are more volatile (more risky) for shorter period. Standard Deviation, a measure of risk is the highest for 1 year returns and lowest for 10 year returns. In simple term longer the period you hold equity class is better.

Having said this, there is also one consideration to this simplistic assumption of long term holding.
